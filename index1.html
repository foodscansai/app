Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the FoodScans Ai Engine?

Our AI Engine provides estimates with approximately 85-90% accuracy for most common foods. Results may vary based on image quality and food complexity.

Can I analyze multiple food items in one image?

Yes! Our analyzer can identify and provide nutritional information for multiple food items in a single image, such as a full meal on a plate.

What types of nutritional information does it provide?

Our analyzer provides calories, macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat), estimated serving size, and additional health insights about the foods identified.

Is this service free to use?

Currently free to use with unlimited analyses. In the future, access will be token-gated, and a limited number of free analyses per day will apply.
